Welcome to 6x17, the latest photographic project by Andrew Norelli.

At its essence, this project is meant to explore the notion of constraints and how they can inspire creative expression. In constraining his medium down to an aspect of 6 by 17, he must view the world from a rather unnatural portal. However, by relieving himself of the task of judging different aspects, he frees himself to explore interesting opportunities by focusing purely on the subjects that fit that aspect.
